Ken Emery is a Registered Professional Builder who has, since 1994, helped build homes for numerous Central Texas Families. Ken has an educational background in Architectural Design and Construction Management. His additional experience in home design contributed to his wealth of industry knowledge. This experience, along with his creative abilities allows him to bring an added dimension to the company.

Our customers can see the difference in Ken’s working knowledge of color, proportion, scale, texture, and materials that translate well to architectural design and construction. His respect for architectural design results in homes that are beautiful and retain the integrity of the neighborhood and land in which they are placed. At every stage of your new home construction, or home remodeling, Ken remains flexible and open to discovering new ways to enhance your home through knowing the direction and desires of his clients.

Clients are impressed with Ken’s broad range of skills which include financial expertise, people management and project scheduling. Good construction management integrates your budget with design. It also involves continual evaluation of materials, construction techniques, and labor to ensure the highest quality home. Finally it involves anticipating problems before they arise and responding efficiently to changes in the field.

Customers come to Concept Home Builders for new home construction, home remodeling and commercial construction. Their projects can be found in the Austin, Georgetown, Salado, Belton, Temple, Harker Heights,and Killeen locations as well as the surrounding area lake shores of Central Texas.
