3001 White Oak Dr

Temple, TX 76502

Boasting a modern design with a sophisticated timeless appeal, this 5,853 sq foot beauty offers a welcome juxtaposition with the wild bamboo growing on the 1.44 acres that is also home to a gurgling creek. Soaring ceilings and walls of windows let the nature around serve as artwork throughout. An exceptional modern kitchen that is perfectly tailored to your lifestyle has an abundance of cabinets, Quartz counters, and stainless steel KitchenAid appliances. The dining option across the kitchen island shares the warmth of a floor to ceiling double sided fireplace that anchors the room. A sceond fireplace is found in the owner's suite bedroom which is flooded with light and views of trees that dapple light through the windows. A rich intensity infuses the owner's suite bath that has a sleek oversized tub and a walk-in shower. Designed with entertaining in mind, the home includes a wet bar with built-in wine refrigerator. Perhaps the most stunning element is the recreation room which boasts an in-ground pool, hot tub, and a multitude of skylights that combine with the wall of windows for an ideal swimming experience. Function and design are perfectly blended to create harmonious spaces that are striking in simplicity and minimalistic in design to bring forth visions of Frank Lloyd Wright's work.
